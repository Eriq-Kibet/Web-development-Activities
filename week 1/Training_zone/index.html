The founder of the company, Terrence Zonas is still CEO andspokesperson for Training Zone. Here is one of his first ever training lessons, the world-famous LectureClash training:
Anyone got a lecture clash? They're not pretty, but with your help, they can be manageable. Here aresome thoughts on how to deal with it:

  • Realise that it's up to you. You are responsible for making it work.
  • Attend one lecture and get the notes from the other (start with the web notes, but that's notenough).
  • Find a friend in the one you miss and get them to tell you what else was covered in class that'snot on the web.
  • Which one do you miss? - Some ideas to help you with that decision are:
    • If you miss one out of two lectures for a subject, that's 50%. If you miss one out of 3that's 33%.
    • Which one are you most on top of?
    • Which subject has the most complete lecture notes?
  • Don't ask your lecturer - "did I miss anything in the lecture?" If you didn't miss anything, there'dbe no point to lectures.
